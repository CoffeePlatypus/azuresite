Grapeful for Grapes

History

Grapeful for grapes was formed when some friends got together and realized that grapes are not appreciated enough. They started meeting monthly while sipping wine and whining about grapes.

That is when Grapeful for Grapes was formed. They got the idea one week when they may have had a little too much wine but it has still proven to be a good idea. Now they meet every week and discuss how to better the world, with grapes.

Mission

Our Mission is to make everyone grapeful for grapes. We would like to help spread grapes around. They are a plant that has been cultivated for centureies and it is our mission to keep that legacy growing. Goal-based, comprehensive, frameworks expediently enable the human resources. We are going to promote a promising trust. Metric and line-of-sight inspire the Managing Director of Business Planning.
