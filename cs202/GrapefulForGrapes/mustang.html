Mustang Grapes

Desctription

The mustang grape vine produces small hard green grapes. When these grapes ripen they turn purple.

Most mustang grapes contain about 4 seeds. The underside of their leaves are white and velvet like.

Botany

Scientific Classification

  • Eukaryote
    • Plantae
      • Vascular Plant
        • Magnoliopsida
          • Vitales
            • Vitaceae
              • Vitis
                • V. mustangensis

The mustang grape is native to the the southern states of the United States. Mustang grapes and leaves are sometimes used to die wool

The mustang grape can cause irrations to the skin. It is also mild unpleasent to eat. This is because the berries are bitter and acidic.
