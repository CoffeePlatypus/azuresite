Fox Grapes

Desctription

Fox grapes are said to have a foxy musk. This is does not mean like the fox animal. This is to describe the earthy and sweet musk from fresh fox grapes.

Most fox grapes are red though there are some white varieties that exist. Their vines tend to have leaves with a hairy underside consisting of brown or while hairs. In some states fox vines can be considered a weed.

Botany

The fox grape vine is native to to eastern Northern America. The concord grape was cultivared from the fox grape so they are pretty similar.

Fox grapes have slip skins. This means when pinched the grape skin easily slips off without crushing the pulp. You could say that fox grapes are slipperly like a fox.
