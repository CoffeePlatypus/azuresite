Grapes

History

Grapes were first cultivated 6,000-8,000 years ago in the Near East.

Types

Amur
Amur grapes are grape at resisting cold. Overall they are tough grapes.
Fox
Fox grapes are known for their slip skin. Also they are said to have a foxy musk
Mustang
Mustand grapes are more bitter and even sometimes irritating to the skin

Uses

Wine
Wine is made by fermenting grapes. People have been making wine for thousands of years. Some people wine that they can not have wine because in the United States one must be 21 years old to purchase wine.
Raisins
Raisins are made by drying out grapes. They can be eater raw or used in various recipes. Some people do not like raisins but there is no good raisin for this as they are a good food.
Jam
Jam is a type a fruit preserve that can be made by jamming the juice a pulp from a grape into a pot then heating it with water and sugar. After that jam is jammed into a container and can later be put on other food.
