Tee Time Shirts

History

Tee Times shirts was founded by a group of graphic design students attending Minneapolis College of Art and Design. It started as a group project for a class. They all liked it so much they decided to build it into a company

They started out just making t-shirts for various clubs at their college. Their shirts were such good quality that they got a fan base They then created a kickstarter which helped them get their business off the ground.

Philosophy

It is our philosophy that we will make everyone the shirt that they want to a T. If our shirts are not made perfectly to your specifications we will replace those shirts and fix what you did not like about the shirt for free.
